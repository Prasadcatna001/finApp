# FinApp - What's New

## 🚀 Key Features
- **Automatic SMS Tracking:** Auto-detects bank transactions from your notifications.
- **Custom SMS Training:** Teach the app how to parse messages from any bank.
- **Lending & Borrowing:** Keep track of debts with friends and colleagues.
- **Bill Splitting:** Easy settlement and tracking for shared expenses.
- **Multi-Account Support:** Manage Banks, Credit Cards, and Wallets in one place.
- **Monthly Activity Feed:** Clear grouping of spending with Income vs Expense summaries.
- **Recurring Payments:** Never miss a bill with automated reminders.
- **Privacy-First:** 100% offline data storage for maximum security.
- **Data Backups:** Export your data to JSON anytime.

## ✨ New in this version
- **Calendar View:** (In-Progress) Displaying day-by-day activities.
- **Built-in Feedback:** Report issues or send reviews directly from settings.
- **Auto-Update Alerts:** Get notified when a new version is ready.
